## TEST CASE 1: OBVIOUS SCAM (Expected: Score 70+, RED)

**Input Type:** Job Description

**Paste this:**
```
URGENT - LIMITED TIME OFFER!!!

Dear Valued Candidate,

Congratulations! You have been pre-selected for an exclusive work-from-home internship 
with our prestigious company. This is a limited-time offer available to only 50 candidates!

Position: Virtual Business Manager
No experience needed! Absolutely no qualifications required!
Earn up to $5,000 per month with minimal effort.

How to Get Started:
1. Send us your bank details
2. Pay a small registration fee of just $200
3. Start working immediately!

This opportunity expires TODAY! Act now or lose this chance forever!

Spots are filling extremely fast. Send your bank account information immediately 
to reserve your position.

Click here to confirm your details urgently.

Best regards,
HR Department
```

**Expected Results:**
- Score: 70-80 (RED)
- Recommendation: REJECT
- Flagged phrases should include:
  - "limited time", "LIMITED TIME"
  - "act now", "Act now"
  - "registration fee"
  - "bank details"
  - "No experience needed"
  - "minimal effort"
  - "expires TODAY"
- Keyword counts:
  - Urgency: 4+
  - Payment: 2+
  - Vagueness: 3+

**What to Say During Demo:**
"Look at this - 4 urgency triggers, payment request, vague job description, and asking for sensitive info. Multiple red flags. Score is red. This is clearly a scam."

## TEST CASE 2: LEGITIMATE JOB (Expected: Score <30, GREEN)

**Input Type:** Job Description

**Paste this:**
```
Accenture Graduate Trainee Program 2026

Job Title: Software Engineer (Graduate Trainee)
Location: Bangalore, India
Work Mode: On-site
Salary: 3.5 - 4.5 LPA (based on performance)

- Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science or related field
- CGPA: 6.5 and above
- Graduation: 2024-2026
- No active backlogs at the time of interview

Our Graduate Trainee Program is designed to develop entry-level software engineers. 
You will work alongside experienced mentors on real-world projects using Java, Python, 
and cloud technologies.

Key Responsibilities:
- Design and develop software solutions
- Participate in code reviews and team discussions
- Complete assigned training modules
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams

Interview Process:
1. Online Aptitude Test (Duration: 1 hour)
2. Technical Interview (45 minutes - Data Structures, Algorithms)
3. HR Round (30 minutes - Culture fit and background)
4. Final Offer (if selected)

Important Information:
- No registration or application fees required
- Training is fully sponsored by Accenture
- All interviews conducted by official recruiters
- Results communicated within 7-10 days

To Apply:
Visit: careers.accenture.com
Email: recruitment-india@accenture.com
Phone: 080-XXXX-XXXX

For more information, visit our official careers page or contact our HR team directly.
```

**Expected Results:**
- Score: 10-25 (GREEN)
- Recommendation: SAFE
- Very few or no flagged phrases
- Keyword counts:
  - Urgency: 0-1
  - Payment: 0
  - Vagueness: 0-1
  - Authority: 0
  - Legitimate: 5+ (qualifications, responsibilities, interview process, training, etc.)

**What to Say During Demo:**

## TEST CASE 3: SUSPICIOUS BUT NOT OBVIOUS (Expected: Score 40-50, YELLOW)

**Input Type:** Email

**Paste this:**
```
Subject: Urgent Reply Needed - Internship Opportunity

Hi There,

I hope this email finds you well. We are hiring enthusiastic candidates for flexible 
remote work positions. No experience or formal qualifications needed - we provide 
complete training!

This is a limited-time recruitment drive. We only have a few spots available, and 
we're moving fast. To secure your position, please reply immediately with:

- Your bank account details
- Your full legal name and ID
- Your address

Verification is quick and takes 2-3 minutes. Once verified, you can start earning 
within 24 hours!

We're especially interested in people who can work part-time or from home. Flexible 
hours available. No boss to answer to!

Interested? Reply ASAP!

Thanks,
Management Team
```

**Expected Results:**
- Score: 45-55 (YELLOW)
- Recommendation: REVIEW
- Flagged phrases:
  - "limited time", "Urgent"
  - "bank account details"
  - "No experience"
  - "remote work", "from home"
  - "ASAP"
- This is a borderline case that deserves review

**What to Say During Demo:**
"This one is trickier. Not obviously a scam, but multiple warning signs. Vague about role, asks for sensitive info, uses urgency. Yellow flag means review carefully before applying."
